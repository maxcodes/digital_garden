## Work

Working on [Recurrente.com](https://recurrente.com), a payments platform based
in Guatemala with the mission to bring economic prosperity to the region.

**AI agents & agentic engineering:** I'm still excited about what the future
holds in agentic engineering. I feel like I'm exploring the frontier of how to
restructure a software engineering org from first principles: how to avoid
fatigue, where human judgment matters most, and how to keep these powerful tools
in check. I recently switched from Claude Code to Codex as my main coding tool,
and I'm enjoying it a bit more. We've written more code in the last month than
in the last five years combined, and it's important for those lines to count for
something. The goal is
to keep building something people want, pace ourselves, listen to users, and
design magical experiences.
